ICDS vs. Ind AS — Construction Contracts & Revenue Recognition

The Income Computation and Disclosure Standards (ICDS) issued under Section 145(2) of the Income Tax Act operate parallel to but independently from the financial reporting standards (Ind AS / AS). A company must maintain both sets of disclosures — one for financial statements, another for tax computation.

The key divergence in construction contracts lies in the percentage-of-completion method: while Ind AS 115 uses a five-step revenue recognition model based on satisfaction of performance obligations
