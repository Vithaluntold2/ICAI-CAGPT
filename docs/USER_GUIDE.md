# 🚀 Getting Started

## Step 1: Open the Website

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
2. Go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org**
3. You will see the home page

---

## Step 2: Create an Account (New Users)

If you don't have an account yet:

1. Click **"Sign up"** link on the login page
2. Fill in the form:
   - **Full Name**: Your name
   - **Email**: Your email address
   - **Password**: Create a strong password
3. Click **"Create Account"** button
4. Check your email for verification link
5. Click the link to verify your email
6. ✅ Your account is now ready!

---

## Step 3: Login to Your Account

1. Go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/auth**
2. Enter your **Email**
3. Enter your **Password**
4. Click **"Sign In"** button
5. ✅ You are now logged in!

---

# 💬 How to Use the Chat

## Step 1: Go to Chat Page

After logging in:
1. You will be on the Chat page automatically
2. Or click **"Chat"** in the navigation menu
3. Or go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/chat**

---

## Step 2: Choose a Chat Mode

At the bottom of the chat, you will see a **mode dropdown**. Click it to choose:

| Mode | What It Does | When to Use |
|------|--------------|-------------|
| 💬 **Standard Chat** | General questions and answers | Quick questions |
| ✨ **Deep Research** | Detailed research with sources | Complex topics needing citations |
| ✅ **Create Checklist** | Creates task lists | Planning and organizing |
| 🔗 **Workflow** | Shows process diagrams | Understanding procedures |
| 📊 **Audit Plan** | Creates audit strategies | Audit planning |
| 🔢 **Financial Calculation** | Tax, NPV, IRR calculations | Number crunching |
| 📈 **Scenario Simulator** | Compares different scenarios | Decision making |
| 📝 **Deliverable Composer** | Creates professional documents | Reports and letters |
| 🔍 **Forensic Intelligence** | Detects fraud patterns | Investigating anomalies |
| 👥 **Roundtable** | Multiple expert opinions | Complex decisions |

---

## Step 3: Ask a Question

1. Type your question in the message box at the bottom
2. Press **Enter** or click the **Send** button
3. Wait for the AI to respond
4. The response appears in the right panel (Output Pane)

### Example Questions:

**For Standard Chat:**
```
What is depreciation and how does it work?
```

**For Financial Calculation:**
```
Calculate NPV for a $100,000 investment at 10% discount rate 
with cash flows of $30,000 per year for 5 years
```

**For Checklist:**
```
Create a month-end close checklist for a small business
```

**For Scenario Simulator:**
```
Compare forming an LLC in Delaware vs California for a tech startup
```

---

## Step 4: Export Your Results

After getting a response:

1. Look at the **right panel** (Output Pane)
2. Click the **Export** button at the top
3. Choose your format:
   - **PDF** - For sharing and printing
   - **Word** - For editing
   - **Excel** - For calculations
   - **PowerPoint** - For presentations
   - **TXT** - Plain text
   - **CSV** - Data tables
4. The file downloads to your computer

---

# 📁 How to Upload Documents

## Step 1: Click the Upload Button

1. In the chat, look for the **📎** (paperclip) icon
2. Click it to open the file picker

---

## Step 2: Select Your File

1. Choose a file from your computer
2. Supported formats: PDF, Word, Excel, images
3. Click **Open**

---

## Step 3: Ask About the Document

1. After uploading, type a question about the document
2. Example: "Summarize this document"
3. Example: "What are the key financial figures in this report?"

# 💳 Subscription Plans

## Free Plan ($0/month)
- 500 queries per month
- 10 documents per month
- 1 profile
- Basic chat modes
- TXT and CSV export only

## Plus Plan ($29/month)
- 3,000 queries per month
- Unlimited documents
- 5 profiles
- All chat modes including Scenario Simulator
- All export formats (PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint)

## Professional Plan ($49/month)
- Unlimited queries
- Unlimited documents
- Unlimited profiles
- All features
- API access
- Forensic Intelligence mode
- White-label reports

## Enterprise Plan (Custom pricing)
- Everything in Professional
- Team management
- SSO/SAML integration
- Custom AI training
- Dedicated support

---

## How to Upgrade Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **"View Plans"** or **"Upgrade"**
3. Or go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/pricing**
4. Select the plan you want
5. Enter payment information
6. Click **"Subscribe"**
7. ✅ Features unlock immediately!

---

# 🔐 Security Features

## How to Enable Two-Factor Authentication (MFA)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
4. Enter the 6-digit code from the app
5. Save your **backup codes** somewhere safe
6. ✅ MFA is now enabled!

## How to Login with MFA

1. Enter your email and password as usual
2. You'll see a screen asking for a 6-digit code
3. Open your authenticator app
4. Enter the current 6-digit code
5. Click **"Verify"**
6. ✅ You're logged in!

# 👑 Admin Features

> ⚠️ **Admin Only** - These features require admin access

## How to Access Admin Panel

1. Login with an admin account
2. Click your **profile icon** (top-right)
3. Click **"Admin Panel"**
4. Or go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/admin**

---

## Admin Dashboard

**What you'll see:**

| Card | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Total Users** | Number of registered users |
| **Active Subscriptions** | Paying customers |
| **Monthly Revenue** | Revenue this month |
| **Queries** | Total AI queries |
| **Documents** | Documents processed |
| **Churn Rate** | Users who cancelled |

---

## How to Manage Users

1. Go to Admin Panel → **Users**
2. Or go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/admin/users**

**What you can do:**

### Change a User's Subscription:
1. Search for the user by name or email
2. Click **"Edit"** on their row
3. Select new tier from dropdown (Free, Plus, Professional, Enterprise)
4. Click **"Save"**

### Make Someone an Admin:
1. Find the user
2. Click **"Make Admin"** button
3. ✅ User now has admin access

### Remove Admin Access:
1. Find the admin user (has 👑 icon)
2. Click **"Remove Admin"**
3. ✅ Admin access removed

---

## How to Manage Coupons

1. Go to Admin Panel → **Coupons**
2. Or go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/admin/coupons**

### Create a New Coupon:
1. Click **"+ Create Coupon"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Code**: The discount code (e.g., SAVE20)
   - **Discount Type**: Percentage or Fixed Amount
   - **Discount Value**: The amount (e.g., 20 for 20%)
   - **Max Uses**: How many times it can be used (optional)
   - **Valid Until**: Expiration date (optional)
3. Toggle **"Active"** to ON
4. Click **"Create Coupon"**
5. ✅ Coupon is now available!

### Deactivate a Coupon:
1. Find the coupon
2. Click **"Edit"**
3. Toggle **"Active"** to OFF
4. Click **"Save"**

---

## How to View Subscriptions

1. Go to Admin Panel → **Subscriptions**
2. Or go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/admin/subscriptions**

**Filter options:**
- By Status: All, Active, Expired, Cancelled
- By Plan: All, Free, Plus, Professional, Enterprise

# 🔧 Super Admin Features

> ⚠️ **Super Admin Only** - Requires email in SUPER_ADMIN_EMAILS environment variable

## How to Access System Monitoring

1. Login as Super Admin
2. Go to Admin Panel
3. Click **"System Monitor"** in the sidebar
4. Or go to: **https://cagpt.icai.org/admin/system-monitoring**

---

## What You'll See in System Monitoring

### System Health Score
- **95-100**: 🟢 Healthy - Everything working
- **70-94**: 🟡 Degraded - Some issues
- **Below 70**: 🔴 Unhealthy - Needs attention

### Resource Metrics
| Metric | What It Shows | Good Value |
|--------|---------------|------------|
| CPU Usage | Server processor load | Below 80% |
| Memory | RAM consumption | Below 85% |
| Uptime | How long server is running | Higher = better |
| Active Alerts | Issues needing attention | 0 is ideal |

### Component Health
Shows status of:
- Database
- Cache
- AI Providers
- File Storage
- WebSocket

---

## How to Deploy a New Version

1. Go to System Monitoring page
2. Click **"Deploy Update"** button (top-right)
3. Fill in:
   - **Version**: New version number (e.g., v1.2.3)
   - **Changes**: List what changed
4. Click **"Start Deployment"**
5. Watch the progress
6. ✅ **Completed** = Success!
7. ❌ **Failed** = Click "Rollback" to undo

---

## How to Schedule Maintenance

1. Go to System Monitoring page
2. Click **"Schedule Maintenance"** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Start Time**: When maintenance begins
   - **End Time**: When it ends
   - **Reason**: Why you're doing maintenance
   - **Affected Services**: Which services will be affected
4. Click **"Schedule Maintenance"**
5. ✅ Maintenance is scheduled!

# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I reset my password?
1. Go to login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"**
3. Enter your email
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click the link
6. Create new password

## Why can't I use Scenario Simulator?
Scenario Simulator requires **Plus plan or higher**. Upgrade your subscription to access it.

## Why can't I export to PDF?
PDF export requires **Plus plan or higher**. Free users can only export to TXT and CSV.

## My account is locked, what do I do?
- Wait 15 minutes for automatic unlock
- Or click "Forgot Password?" to reset and unlock

## How do I check my remaining queries?
Go to **Settings** → **Subscription** to see your usage.
